Title: Agile Antelope

Date: 7 September 2023

A living human's body and a dead eland's head. Life and death, bio-alchemically transmuted into one entity. The eland is a symbol of grace, speed, and power. Upon its forehead is the silhouette of the winged thunderbolt, symbolic of both speed and power. The human body has a green and gold tint to it, implying both sickness and vitality. The background is a sickly green, another nod towards death, while simultaneously green being the colour of life and growth. In future I hope to add more symbolism into these pieces, and initially sketched out the idea of a living eagle having its wings pierced by the eland's horns, but decided against it to stick to my deadline.

Title: Battleworn (Teddy) Bear

Date: 10 September 2023

A bear with a clipped ear, scarred over from old conflict. The bear's snout has been cut off, showing the laughing human skull, hiding beneath the surface. Upon the human skull the constellation Ursa Major has been tattooed. Above the bear is a crescent moon, indicative of the lunar qualities of bears.
