Juliet

Hi, I am Juliet. Writing has been something that has been lurking over my shoulder for quite a while now, and I think now is the time for me to try it out! Perhaps it is specific moments in one's life that defines them - this is what I am going to explore. My goal is to tell a story through descriptive peices of moments in a person's life. She will be fantastical and beautiful, ugly and cruel, but hopefully once all is said and done - she will be whole.

JARROD

Hi, I'm Jarrod van Schalkwyk, and I am working on honing my digital art skills, specifically in the style of oil painting. For the Alphabet Superset I will be focusing on the theme of Mutation, specifically in the sense of human and animal combinations both in a scientific and fantasy sense. This theme is important to me as I am heavily influenced by fantastical creatures, but also by the spiritual connotations of them.

Sean

Hello! I'm Sean, and I have recently taken a liking to writing a little poetry! My hope for this project is to weave together my poems with some digital sketching, and try to explore a new medium for my creativity that I haven't really attempted before. The poems I'm hoping to write here are intended as an exploration of animism - the practice of seeing the human in the more-than-human world. I hope they're fun to read and prod you in the feely bits occasionally!
