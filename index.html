JARROD

Hi, I'm Jarrod van Schalkwyk, and I am working on honing my digital art skills, specifically in the style of oil painting. For the Alphabet Superset I will be focusing on the them of Mutation, specifically in the sense of human and animal combinations both in a scientific and fantasy sense. This theme is important to me as I am heavily influenced by fantastical creatures, but also by the spiritual connotations of them.
